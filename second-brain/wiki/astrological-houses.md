# Astrological houses

The **houses** are twelve divisions of the chart, each governing an area of life
(self, money, communication, home, …). Where [[zodiac-sign|signs]] describe *how*
a planet acts, houses describe *where* in life it plays out.

## Detail

A [[birth-chart]] places each of [[the-planets]] into one of the twelve houses.
The house layout is anchored by the [[ascendant]]: the sign rising at birth
becomes the 1st house cusp.

[[astroprecise]] uses the **Whole Sign** (equal house) system — each house is one
full 30° sign, starting from the Ascendant's sign. This is the simplest of the
several competing house systems in astrology.

## Connections

- Anchored by the [[ascendant]].
- Hold [[the-planets]] in a [[birth-chart]], alongside [[zodiac-sign|signs]].
