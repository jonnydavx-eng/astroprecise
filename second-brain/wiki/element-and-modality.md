# Element and modality

Every [[zodiac-sign]] is classified by one **element** (4) and one **modality**
(3). The 4 × 3 grid yields exactly 12 unique combinations — one per sign — which
is why the zodiac has twelve members.

## Detail

**Elements (temperament):**
- **Fire** — energetic, assertive (Aries, Leo, Sagittarius)
- **Earth** — grounded, practical (Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn)
- **Air** — intellectual, social (Gemini, Libra, Aquarius)
- **Water** — emotional, intuitive (Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces)

**Modalities (mode of action):**
- **Cardinal** — initiates (the signs that open each season)
- **Fixed** — stabilises and sustains
- **Mutable** — adapts and transitions

Each element appears in all three modalities; each modality appears in all four
elements — so no two signs share both.

## Connections

- Classifies every [[zodiac-sign]].
- Part of what makes up a [[birth-chart]] reading.
