# Second brain

A **second brain** is an external, searchable store of the ideas you've
encountered, organised so you can retrieve and connect them later instead of
relying on memory. Here it takes the form of a folder of linked Markdown notes
that an AI agent maintains for you.

## Detail

The shift the idea proposes: use AI not primarily to *write code*, but to
*curate knowledge*. You supply raw material; the agent does the librarian
work — reading, distilling, linking, and filing — so the collection stays
organised as it grows.

Three moving parts make it run:

- **`raw/`** — where you drop sources (articles, transcripts, PDFs).
- **`wiki/`** — the agent's output: clean, linked notes.
- **`CLAUDE.md`** — the standing instructions that make ingestion consistent.

The payoff: you "never start from a blank chat again," because every question is
asked *against your accumulated notes*.
