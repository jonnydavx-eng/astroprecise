# Ascendant

The **Ascendant** (or **Rising sign**) is the [[zodiac-sign]] that was on the
eastern horizon at the exact moment of birth. It's one of the **Big Three**
placements of a [[birth-chart]] and is read as the outward persona — the "front
door" through which a person meets the world.

## Detail

Unlike the Sun sign, the Ascendant depends on **birth time and place**, not just
the date — it advances through all twelve signs roughly every 24 hours, about one
sign every two hours. This is why an accurate chart needs a birth time.

It also anchors the [[astrological-houses]]: the rising sign becomes the cusp of
the 1st house, setting the whole house layout.

[[birth-chart-calculator|AstroPrecise computes it]] from **Local Sidereal Time +
latitude**.
