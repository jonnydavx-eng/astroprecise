# Zodiac sign

The **zodiac** is the band of sky divided into **12 signs** of 30° each. Every
sign carries an [[element-and-modality|element and modality]] and a ruling
[[the-planets|planet]], which together give it its character.

## Detail

| Sign | Dates | Element | Modality | Ruler |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Aries ♈ | Mar 21–Apr 19 | Fire | Cardinal | Mars |
| Taurus ♉ | Apr 20–May 20 | Earth | Fixed | Venus |
| Gemini ♊ | May 21–Jun 20 | Air | Mutable | Mercury |
| Cancer ♋ | Jun 21–Jul 22 | Water | Cardinal | Moon |
| Leo ♌ | Jul 23–Aug 22 | Fire | Fixed | Sun |
| Virgo ♍ | Aug 23–Sep 22 | Earth | Mutable | Mercury |
| Libra ♎ | Sep 23–Oct 22 | Air | Cardinal | Venus |
| Scorpio ♏ | Oct 23–Nov 21 | Water | Fixed | Pluto |
| Sagittarius ♐ | Nov 22–Dec 21 | Fire | Mutable | Jupiter |
| Capricorn ♑ | Dec 22–Jan 19 | Earth | Cardinal | Saturn |
| Aquarius ♒ | Jan 20–Feb 18 | Air | Fixed | Uranus |
| Pisces ♓ | Feb 19–Mar 20 | Water | Mutable | Neptune |

The **Sun sign** is the one most people know — determined by birth date alone
(`ZodiacSign.fromBirthDate(month, day)`). A full [[birth-chart]] places *every*
planet in a sign, not just the Sun.

## Connections

- Each sign expresses an [[element-and-modality]] pairing.
- Each sign is ruled by one of [[the-planets]].
- Signs are one axis of a [[birth-chart]]; [[astrological-houses]] are the other.
