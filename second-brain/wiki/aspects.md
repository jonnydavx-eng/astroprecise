# Aspects

An **aspect** is an angular relationship between two of [[the-planets]] in a
[[birth-chart]]. When the angle between two planets is close to certain values,
they're said to be "in aspect" and to interact. [[astroprecise]] computes the
five **Ptolemaic (major) aspects**.

## Detail

| Aspect | Symbol | Angle | Orb | Nature |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Conjunction | ☌ | 0° | 8° | Neutral |
| Sextile | ⚹ | 60° | 4° | Harmonious |
| Square | □ | 90° | 8° | Challenging |
| Trine | △ | 120° | 8° | Harmonious |
| Opposition | ☍ | 180° | 8° | Challenging |

- **Orb** = how far from the exact angle the aspect still counts. A Sextile only
  holds within 4°; the others allow 8°.
- **Nature** = the flavour: *harmonious* aspects (sextile, trine) flow easily;
  *challenging* ones (square, opposition) create tension; a conjunction is
  *neutral* — it intensifies whatever the two planets mean together.
- An aspect can be **applying** (angle tightening toward exact) or separating.

## Connections

- Relate [[the-planets]] within a [[birth-chart]].
- Computed by the [[birth-chart-calculator]].
