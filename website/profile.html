Sync with the AstroPrecise Android App

Transfer your saved charts to the Android app in three easy steps:

  1. 1. Export your data from the Export / Import section to download astroprecise-profile.json
  2. 2. Transfer the file to your Android device via USB, Google Drive, or any file sharing method.
  3. 3. Import in the app — open AstroPrecise on Android, go to Profile → Import, and select the file.

Privacy by Design

Why Local-First?

We believe your personal data — especially something as intimate as your birth chart — should belong entirely to you.

🔒

No Account Required

There is no login, no password, and no user account. Your profile exists entirely in your browser's local storage — invisible to us.

🌐

Works Offline

Once the page has loaded, all calculations and chart rendering happen entirely in your browser — no network requests needed.

📦

Portable JSON Format

The export format is open and human-readable. Inspect, modify, or migrate your chart data freely — no vendor lock-in ever.
