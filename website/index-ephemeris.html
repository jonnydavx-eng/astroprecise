On accuracy & privacy

Common questions

  • 01

    How accurate are the planetary positions?

    Astro Precise uses VSOP87, a truncated series accurate to within one arc-second over several centuries. For chart purposes this exceeds any printed ephemeris.

  • 02

    Does my birth data leave my device?

    No. All computation happens in your browser. Nothing is sent to a server, stored in a database, or attached to an account. Close the tab and it is gone.

  • 03

    Can I see the code?

    Yes. The engine is unminified JavaScript — open browser DevTools, find js/ephemeris.js, and read it. The math is inline. No black box.

  • 04

    Do I need an account for the free tools?

    No account is needed for the chart, the sky, or the daily horoscope. Paid readings require an email address so we can deliver the PDF — that is the only data we hold.
