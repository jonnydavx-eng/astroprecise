Name Numerology — Frequently Asked Questions

What is name numerology?

Name numerology assigns a number to each letter of your name and reduces the totals to reveal core numbers. The Pythagorean system maps A=1 through I=9, then repeats J=1 through R=9 and S=1 through Z=8. From your full name it derives your Expression (Destiny) number from all letters, your Soul Urge from the vowels, and your Personality number from the consonants.

How do you calculate your numerology number from your name?

Convert each letter of your full name to its Pythagorean value, add them up, then reduce the total by summing its digits until you reach a single digit (1 to 9) or a master number (11, 22, or 33). For the Soul Urge use only the vowels; for the Personality number use only the consonants. This calculator shows every step so you can verify the arithmetic yourself.

What is the difference between the Expression, Soul Urge, and Personality numbers?

The Expression (or Destiny) number comes from all the letters of your full name and describes your natural talents and life direction. The Soul Urge (or Heart's Desire) number comes from the vowels and reflects your inner motivations and what you truly want. The Personality number comes from the consonants and describes the impression you make on others.

What are master numbers in numerology?

11, 22, and 33 are master numbers. When a total reduces to one of these, it is left unreduced rather than being collapsed to a single digit, because master numbers are considered to carry a heightened, more demanding vibration. 11 is the Illuminator, 22 the Master Builder, and 33 the Master Teacher.

Which name should I use for numerology?

Most numerologists use the full name given at birth as it appears on the birth certificate, because it is considered the blueprint of your potential. You can also calculate the numbers for a name you currently use to see the energy that name carries day to day. The calculator is deterministic, so the same name always returns the same numbers.
