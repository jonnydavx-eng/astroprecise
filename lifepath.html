      The Method

      How It Works

      I

      Reduce Each Component

      The month, day, and year of your birth are each reduced to a single digit by summing their component numbers. A birth on 29 January: day 2+9=11 (a Master Number — held here, not reduced further).

      II

      Honour Master Numbers

      11, 22, and 33 are Master Numbers. They carry higher vibrational intensity and are never reduced to 2, 4, or 6 in the final calculation. If they appear at any stage of reduction, they are preserved.

      III

      Sum and Reduce Again

      The three reduced components are added together. If the result exceeds 9 (and is not 11, 22, or 33), sum the digits again. The final single digit or Master Number is your Life Path.

      Example Calculation

      Birth date: 14 April 1992
      Month: 4
      Day: 1+4 = 5
      Year: 1+9+9+2 = 21 → 2+1 = 3
      Life Path: 4 + 5 + 3 = 12 → 1+2 = 3
