## The one truth that decides speed
You already built the funnel (free accurate chart), the fulfilment engine (`tools/generate-reading.mjs` turns any birth data into a sellable PDF in seconds), and the wiring (`window.AP_MON` — paste a URL, the surface goes live; blank = nothing renders). So **time-to-first-pound is gated by creating an account + pasting a link, not by building.** Tips ≈ 20 min. A paid product ≈ 1–2 hrs. After that, it's all **traffic** — the fastest pound comes from warm traffic you can reach today (Reddit, personal socials), not cold SEO.

## ★ DO THESE 3 FIRST (highest pound-per-hour, all day-one)
1. **Ko-fi tip jar** (`tipUrl`) — 0% on tips, ~20 min, zero fulfilment, the only rail GitHub Pages *explicitly* permits, perfectly on-brand ("keep the sky free"). First pound can land the same day you share the tool.
2. **£18 Deep Reading on Lemon Squeezy** (`deepReadingUrl`) — your hero product + solid margin (~£16 net). Generator produces it; LS is merchant-of-record (handles your VAT). Manual-fulfil within 24h. One sale ≈ 9 tips.
3. **£9 print-at-home poster PDF** (`posterUrl`) — the low-friction first paid step + highest *velocity* (Pinterest-native, ~£8 net). Free chart → £9 poster is a tiny leap that converts curiosity into a first transaction.

> Together they cover impulse / considered / altruistic buyers — the whole first-purchase spectrum — and all go live the moment you paste a URL. **Skip bundle, gift, POD and affiliate on day one.**
